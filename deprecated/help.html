Instructions

  1. Allow the browser to access your MIDI devices.
  2. Connect your Mercury7 to the MIDI adapter.
  3. Select input & output devices and MIDI channel.
  4. Move a knob on your Mercury7, the corresponding on-screen control must move accordingly.
    • To move the knobs, use the mouse.
    • Click and drag over the knob. You can also use the mousewheel.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • ESC: close dialogs and settings
  • 0..9: presets 1..10
  • Right, +: next preset
  • Left, -: previous preset
  • Alt: keep pressed to view raw values
  • SPACE BAR: BYPASS on/off
  • S: SWELL
  • I: Init
  • R: Randomize
  • PageUp: EXP max
  • PageDn: EXP min
  • TAB: toggle EXP values
  • Shift: show EXP values
  • Up, Dn: animate exp
Use Ctrl to play animations until 50%.
