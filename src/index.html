Instructions

  1. Allow the browser to access your MIDI devices.
  2. If it's not already done, connect your Mercury7 to its MIDI adapter.
  3. When the Mercury7 is connected the logo will glow.
  4. Move a knob on your Mercury7, the corresponding on-screen control must move accordingly.

Knobs:

  • To move the knobs, use the mouse.
  • Click and drag over the knob. You can also use the mousewheel.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • ESC: close dialogs and settings
  • SPACE BAR: Bypass on/off
  • 0..9: presets 1..10
  • Up, Right, +: next preset
  • Down, Left, -: previous preset
  • T: TAP
  • I: Init
  • R: Randomize
  • D,A,M,P: Dry, Arp, Mono, Poly
  • W: Sawtooth wave
  • Q: Square wave
  • C, V: animate pitch
  • B, N: animate sustain
  • F, G: animate filter
  • H, J: animate filter Q
  • Y, X: animate mix
  • K, L: animate delay level
  • Z, U: animate ring mod
  • -, +: animate modulation
  • O: animate portamento
  • DEL: animate sustain to 0
Use shift to play animation until 50%.
